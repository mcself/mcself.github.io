an enormous challenge about me

Posted on 2019-04-17 | Edited on 2019-04-20 | In Ming's think , record | Comments: | Views:
Symbols count in article: 3.1k | Reading time ≈ 3 mins.

Break out crank one day，I want to try a challenge that write my blog with full English.

Occasionally I found a website for vocabulary test, and found that the vocabulary only stayed less than 3000 words. It reminds me that I haven’t learned English well since I was in college. I have been fishing for four years. I am ashamed that I haven’t passed any CET4/CET6.

hexo 固定连接

In fact, I also want to learn a foreign language well first. A while ago, from familiar English, I memorized almost a month’s words, but the effect was not very good. So suddenly, I want to try to start blogging in English and improve my English level. Blog content may be the triviality of life, the heart of learning and personal thinking.

As a beginner, it must be difficult to write a full English blog. But there is a goal, a direction is better than aimless learning. Referring to the time division of “Wandering Earth”, we first set up the next Flag: the first stage, the sea of words stage, writing Chinese, translating English; the second stage, the primary stage, writing English, translating Chinese; the third stage, the Big Bang stage, reading a large number of English articles, writing in-depth content.

Stage of word sea

The word sea stage is the accumulation stage of acquiring vocabulary, learning grammar and learning common sentence patterns. The preliminary plan is to try to write Chinese documents before translating them into English. Because of the lack of vocabulary, in the process of translation, I will try to look up dictionaries, grammar and collocations as much as possible to ensure the minimum error rate. In order to accumulate a large number of vocabulary, grammar, sentence patterns and so on. So at this stage, there must be many simple, low-level grammatical errors and even spelling errors in the article. Regardless of him, first write, anyway, for the most primitive accumulation.

Native stage

The primary stage is the necessary stage to produce English thinking and to use English well. At this stage, with the accumulation of vocabulary, grammar and sentence patterns, basic English writing can begin. In my opinion, without Chinese, pure English can make better use of the accumulation made in the first stage. Only by thinking and writing with the usual writing logic of English can we feel the charm of different languages more deeply. Later, translating articles in Chinese in a speaker’s way can better understand the different logic of thinking. In the meantime, there may be more gains, which will be at the ideological level of talent.

Big Bang Phase

The Big Bang stage is the final inspection stage. According to the plan, after the first two stages, whether writing Chinese or writing English, reading Chinese or reading English, there should be a qualitative improvement. So at this time, I may read and write more. In content, it will not be confined to daily life, in the field of vision of writing topics, will be more open.

After the stage is over, if it works, maybe I will try other languages. Because I think that only by learning a language well, can we better understand a culture.
