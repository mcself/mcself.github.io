An Enormous Challenge About Me

Posted on 2019-04-17 | Edited on 2019-04-20 | In Ming's think , record | Comments: | Views:
Symbols count in article: 3k | Reading time ≈ 3 mins.

The other day I did a vocabulary test on a website witch be named Test your vocab and found that my vocabulary stayed at the level of less than 3000.I am ashamed that I indulged in slacking instead of learned English in college and haven’t take the CET4/6 exam.So I want to try a challenge that write my blog all in English to improve the level of my English.

In practice,I also want to learn a foreign language well first. Remember the English words has already been started a while ago but the result is unsatisfactory. So suddenly, I want to try to start blogging all in English.Maybe the blog will share something about my livelihood, experiences in learning and my personal thinking.

As a newbie,it must be difficult to blogging all in English.But if there is a goal, a direction,it’s better than without purpose.Reference to the Time Division of《wandering earth》，I set my mind to learning:the first phase I called The Words Phase, writing Chinese and translating it into English; the second phase be named The Essence Phase,writing English and translating it into Chinese;the third phase is The Big Bang,reading a lot of English articles and write in depth conetent.

The Words Phase

The words phase is the stage that to expanding vocabulary, learning grammar and common sentence patterns. The tentative plan is translating the documents what be wrote by Chinese. Because of the limited vocabulary,in the process of translation, I will try to consult dictionaries, grammar and collocations,as much as possible to ensure the smallest mistakes and accumulating a great deal of vocabulary, grammar, sentence patterns and so on. At this phase, there must be many simple, low-level grammatical errors and even spelling errors in the article. Regardless, doing it, for the most primitive accumulation(原始积累).

The Essence Phase

The Essence Phase is the necessary stage to produce logical forms of English and to full use of English. At this phase,I think I can trying basic English writing with accumulation of vocabulary, grammar and sentence patterns.It can make better use of the accumulation made in the first phase when I blogging all in English rather than use writing Chinese and translating it into English.Only by thinking and writing with the usual writing logic of English just might feel the fascination of different languages more deeply.Later, translating articles in Chinese in practice better than understand the different logic of thinking. Meanwhile, there may be more gains at the ideological level.

The Big Bang

The Big Bang stage is the final test phase. According to the plan, after The words phase,The Essence Phase, whether writing Chinese or English, reading Chinese or English, there should be a qualitative improvement. So at this time.Maybe I will reading and writing more. In content, it will not be confined to daily life.From the Perspective of Writing Topic Selection, will be more widest.

If I can finish it, if it worked for me, maybe I will try to learn other languages.
